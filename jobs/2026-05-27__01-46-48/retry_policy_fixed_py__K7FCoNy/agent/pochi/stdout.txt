## What each file does

- **`activities.py`** — `flaky_task` writes `Attempt <N>` to `/workspace/attempts.log` on every invocation, then raises `ApplicationError("boom")`.
- **`workflows.py`** — `FlakyWorkflow` calls `flaky_task` with `RetryPolicy(maximum_attempts=5, initial_interval=2s, backoff_coefficient=1.0)` (fixed 2-second delay between retries), catches `ActivityError` after retries are exhausted, and returns `"failed after 5 attempts"`.
- **`worker.py`** — Connects to Temporal Cloud via `TEMPORAL_ADDRESS`, `TEMPORAL_NAMESPACE`, `TEMPORAL_API_KEY` (with TLS), registers `FlakyWorkflow` and `flaky_task` on task queue `retry-py`.
- **`client.py`** — Starts the workflow with ID `retry-py-${ZEALT_RUN_ID}`, waits for the result, and writes `Workflow result: failed after 5 attempts` to `/workspace/result.log`.
- **`run.sh`** — Launches the worker in the background, waits 3 seconds for it to connect, runs the client, then shuts down the worker.
