If `deposit` fails, the workflow must execute a `refund` compensation step and then fail the workflow, leaving account balances consistent. The workflow is exercised against **Temporal Cloud** (provided via `TEMPORAL_ADDRESS`, `TEMPORAL_NAMESPACE`, and `TEMPORAL_API_KEY` env vars). You MUST connect to the real Temporal Cloud — do **not** mock the Temporal SDK or run a local server.
