The workflow is exercised against **Temporal Cloud** (provided via `TEMPORAL_ADDRESS`, `TEMPORAL_NAMESPACE`, and `TEMPORAL_API_KEY` env vars). You MUST connect to the real Temporal Cloud — do **not** mock the Temporal SDK or run a local server.
